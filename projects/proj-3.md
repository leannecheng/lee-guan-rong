**作品简介：**
<br>

这幅作品创作于2023年。书写的主要目的是为了练习草书，尝试让线条表现得更加生动、富有流动感。在选择书写内容时，我选用了白居易的《夜雪》，诗中描写身处寒冷中通过感官感知周围环境的意境，正好能与草书那种连绵起伏、灵动变化的笔墨节奏相呼应，从而在视觉上呈现出一种清冷且细腻的氛围。

基本信息：



内容：白居易《夜雪》

年份：2023

书体：草书

格式：四开条幅（卷轴装裱）


<br>
**Description:**

Created in 2023, the primary goal of this piece was to practice Cursive Script, focusing on making the strokes more vivid and fluid. I chose Bai Juyi’s poem "Night Snow" as the content because its sensory depiction of a cold night resonates with the rhythmic flow of cursive calligraphy. The aim was to use the lively movement of the ink to capture the quiet, chilly atmosphere described in the poem.

Details:



Content: Bai Juyi's "Night Snow"

Year: 2023

Script Style: Cursive Script

Format: 4-cut scroll
