**作品简介：**
<br>

此作创作于2025年。创作初衷是为了研习沈体（沈慕羽风格）那份独有的宽博厚重与正气凛然。为了配合这种端庄雄健的字体风格，我特意选取了李白《行路难·其一》中气势豪迈的诗句：“长风破浪会有时，直挂云帆济沧海”。作品采用四尺对开条幅并配合卷轴装裱，力求在笔墨的方正之间，展现出诗词与字体相得益彰的力量感。

基本信息：



内容：李白《行路难·其一》（节选）

年份：2025

书体：楷书（沈体 - 沈慕羽风格）

格式：四尺对开条幅（卷轴装裱）


<br>
**Description:**

Created in 2025, the primary purpose of this piece was to practice the Shen style (Sim Mow Yu's style), known for its broad, thick, and righteous character structure. To complement this dignified and vigorous aesthetic, I selected the powerful lines from Li Bai's poem "Hard is the Road": "A time will come to ride the wind and cleave the waves, I'll set my cloud-white sail and cross the sea which raves." Mounted as a 4-foot half-cut scroll, the work aims to harmonize the resolute nature of the calligraphy with the heroic spirit of the text.

Details:



Content: Excerpt from Li Bai's "Hard is the Road"

Year: 2025

Script Style: Standard Script (Shen / Sim Mow Yu Style)

Format: 4-foot half-cut scroll
