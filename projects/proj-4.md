**作品简介：**
<br>

这幅作品创作于2022年，内容是韦应物的《秋夜寄丘二十二员外》。这是我一次尝试将情感深度注入米芾风格行书的创作。米芾的字通常风樯阵马、沉着痛快，但我在此作中试图放慢心境，去体悟诗人“怀君属秋夜，散步咏凉天”的那份静谧与思念。我希望通过行书的牵丝映带和墨色变化，表现出秋夜的清凉以及诗人独自徘徊时内心的波澜，让行书不仅有技巧的展示，更有一份真挚的情感流露。

**Description:**

Artwork 5: To Assessment Officer Qiu on an Autumn Night

Created in 2022, this scroll features Wei Yingwu's poem "To Assessment Officer Qiu on an Autumn Night." This piece represents my exploration into infusing deep emotion into the Mi Fu style of Running Script. While Mi Fu's style is often characterized by vigorous and dynamic strokes, I aimed to channel the poem's quiet solitude—"Thinking of you on this autumn night, walking alone chanting in the cool air." Through the subtle connections between strokes and variations in ink, I strove to capture the coolness of the autumn night and the gentle fluctuation of the poet's thoughts, making the calligraphy a vessel for genuine feeling.
