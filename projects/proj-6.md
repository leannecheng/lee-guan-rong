**作品简介**：
<br>

这是龙年春节期间为亲戚的生意特别书写的春联。内容采用了传统的七言联“龙狮腾飞生意旺，春光明媚财源道”，横批“金玉满堂”。使用楷书书写，主打清晰端正，作为一份节日的祝福送给亲人，祝愿其生意兴隆。

基本信息：



内容：龙狮腾飞生意旺，春光明媚财源道（横批：金玉满堂）

书体：楷书

用途：龙年亲友赠礼（商业祝福）


<br>
**Description:**

This couplet was written as a gift for a relative's business during the Dragon Year Lunar New Year. The text, "Dragon and Lion soar, business prospers; Spring is bright, wealth flows," is written in clear Standard Script (Kai). It serves as a straightforward, traditional holiday blessing for commercial success.

Details:



Content: Dragon Year Business Couplet

Script Style: Standard Script

Purpose: Gift for Relatives (Business Blessing)

内容：乙巳春来添喜气，家门福至纳瑞祥（横批：福运安康）

用途：蛇年亲友赠礼（居家祝福）


<br>
**Description:**

Created to welcome the Yi Si Year of the Snake, this couplet was written for a relative's home. The text reads, "The Yi Si spring arrives adding joy; Blessings reach the family door welcoming auspiciousness." Written in Running Script (Xing) to add a touch of liveliness suitable for the festival, it is a simple gesture of well-wishing for the family's happiness and health.

Details:



Content: Snake Year Home Couplet

**作品简介**：
<br>


这是一次充满趣味的创意尝试，源于校园举办的新春红纸设计活动。我在A4大小的红纸上，将传统的吉祥话通过合体字的形式重新解构与组合。作品包含了“逢考必过”与“金玉满堂”两个合体字，既寄托了学生时代对学业顺利的期盼，也送上了对财富充盈的美好祝愿。为了使画面构图更加平衡，我在左下角特别添加了一个“福”字，不仅填补了空间的留白，更增添了一份圆满与福气。

基本信息：



内容：合体字（逢考必过、金玉满堂、福）

年份：校园活动创作

书体：创意合体字

格式：A4红纸


<br>
**Description:**

This piece reflects a playful and creative experiment undertaken during a school Lunar New Year design activity. On a sheet of red paper, I deconstructed and recombined traditional auspicious phrases into Combined Characters (Ligatures). The design features "Pass Every Exam" (逢考必过) and "Gold and Jade Fill the Hall" (金玉满堂), blending wishes for academic success with hopes for prosperity. To achieve visual balance, I added the character "Fu" (Blessing) in the bottom left corner, which not only stabilizes the composition but also adds a finishing touch of good fortune.

Details:



Content: Combined Characters (Pass Exams / Wealth / Blessing)

Year: School Event Creation

Script Style: Creative Combined Script

Format: A4 Red Paper
