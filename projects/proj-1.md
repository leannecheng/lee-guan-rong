**作品简介：**
<br>

此幅《百福图》创作于2024年，汇集了楷、行、隶、篆、草五种核心书体，并融入了古老的甲骨文元素，展现了“福”字在汉字演变长河中的千姿百态。创作初衷既是为了给家中增添一份祥瑞与福气，也是为了在笔墨转换间，磨练对不同书体结构的驾驭能力与应对技巧。作品选用四尺四开宣纸书写，虽仅作简单塑封保护而未进行繁复装裱，却保留了墨迹最原本的质朴与真诚，寄托了对生活最纯粹的祝福。

基本信息：



内容：百福图（集五体与甲骨文）

年份：2024

书体：楷书、行书、隶书、篆书、草书、甲骨文

格式：四尺四开宣纸（简单塑封）


<br>
**Description:**

Created in 2024, this "Hundred Fu" artwork integrates five major calligraphy scripts—Standard (Kai), Running (Xing), Clerical (Li), Seal (Zhuan), and Cursive (Cao)—along with elements of the ancient Oracle Bone script. It showcases the diverse evolution of the character "Fu" (Blessing). The piece was created to bring good fortune to the household while serving as a study to refine the command over various script structures. Written on 4-foot 4-cut Xuan paper and preserved with simple lamination rather than traditional mounting, it presents the ink in its most authentic state, embodying a sincere and pure blessing for life.

Details:



Content: Hundred Fu (Variations of the character "Blessing")

Year: 2024

Script Style: Standard, Running, Clerical, Seal, Cursive, and Oracle Bone Scripts

Format: 4-foot 4-cut Xuan paper (Simple lamination)
